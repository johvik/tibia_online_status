Red Rose

We are an ancient guild of Good, followers of Banor. We are peaceloving and try to uphold law and noble virtues, but like the rose we have sharp thorns, too! - Founded December 7th 1998 - - Proud winner of the first ever Tibian Football League - Recruitment is currently open! Contact us at our forum, or at contact@redrose-guild.com

The guild was founded on Antica on Feb 18 2002.
It is currently active.
The official homepage is at redrose-guild.com.
Their home on Antica is Guildhall of the Red Rose. The rent is paid until Sep 12 2014.
