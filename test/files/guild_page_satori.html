Satori

Guild Information
As the world moves onward into darkness and the smell of blood is thick in the air, we strive to hold back the powers of the night. Justice and order shall be established once again, chaos shall be driven back into its murky lair. As we give light, so shall we receive light. ((Applications via Satori home page))

The guild was founded on Antica on Feb 18 2002.
It is currently active.
Guild is opened for applications.
The official homepage is at www.satoriguild.net/satoriforum/index.php.
Their home on Antica is Castle of the Winds. The rent is paid until Dec 22 2017.
